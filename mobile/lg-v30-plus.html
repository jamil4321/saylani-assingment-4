LG V30 Plus

LG V30 Plus - Inspire, Create, Share
LG brings new variant named V30 Plus and it is going to launch on 13th of December in Pakistan. This company is very popular just like the Samsung and both of them are the Korean tech giant. LG V30 Plus is successor of its elder brother which was flagship device of the company. A detailed specification list of the device is leaked which all the specs of the LG's V30 Plus and provide the idea that what is special about the upcoming device. As usual this brand has focused on quality of its products that's why the outer shell of the V30 Plus by LG is quite attractive and solid. Display department got full support of OLED panels packed inside the massive 6 inches diagonals. Following the latest trends display of LG Plus support Full Vision display where the ratio of the display screen is 9:18 and the resolution of the display is 1440 x 2160 pixel while the pixel density of the display is 537 pixels per inch. LG V30 Plus's Snapdragon 835 chipset is quite popular in gadget world which has created too much hype in mobile world. Along with this chipset Adreno 540 GPU is planted inside the LG V30's Plus to make sure that chipset did not get too messy while running heavy graphics application like games or anything else. 4GB of RAM is used to manage the V30 Plus's multi-tasking while internal storage of the smartphone is 128 GB which is backed up by the 256 GB of micro SD card. 3300 mAh battery using Qualcomm Quick charge technology where LG V30 Plus also support wireless charging just like the Samsung Galaxy Note 8 which is the also a flagship smartphone..
